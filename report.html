Abstract

In June 2020 New Zealand's governments announce the country covid-free. While, it didn't last long the New Zealand is still one of the country with low covid-19 cases and covid-related deaths. In this report we will focus on comparing the covid-19 cases in New Zealand to other countries and try to analyze government's response to the pandemic. Additionally we will shows the demographic of covid-19 patients and how the pandemic influences different regions of New Zealand's economy.
